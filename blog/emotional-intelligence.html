Emotional Intelligence

A Crash Course on Emotion Intelligence

What is Emotional intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is the ability to manage both your own emotions and understand the emotions of people around you.

Elements that indicate emotional intelligence

  • An ability to identify and describe what people are feeling
  • An awareness of personal strengths and limitations
  • Self-confidence and self-acceptance
  • The ability to let go of mistakes
  • An ability to accept and embrace change
  • A strong sense of curiosity, particularly about other people
  • Feelings of empathy, sensitivity and concern for others
  • Accepting responsibility for mistakes
  • The ability to manage emotions in difficult situations

How is EQ different to IQ?

  • EQ refers to one's ability to recognise and regulate emotions.
  • EQ focuses on ones emotions in varies ways:
    • Perceiving emotions
    • Reasoning with emotions
    • Understanding emotions
    • Managing emotions
  • IQ refers to how quickly one can solve problems with reasoning

Why is EQ important?

EQ's importance in the modern world has evolved and has overtaken IQ. It's affects can be seen in out home and work lives. EQ is what gives us social and self awareness. Whether online or in person, at work or at home, EQ can help each of us build and maintain stronger and better relationships.
