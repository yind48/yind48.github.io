Emotional Intelligence

A Crash Course on Emotion Intelligence

What is Emotional intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is the ability to manage both your own emotions and understand the emotions of people around you.

How is EQ different to IQ?

  • EQ refers to one's ability to recognise and regulate emotions
  • IQ refers to how quickly one can solve problems with reasoning

Why is EQ important?

EQ's importance in the modern world has evolved and has overtaken IQ. It's affects can be seen in out home and work lives. EQ is what gives us social and self awareness.
