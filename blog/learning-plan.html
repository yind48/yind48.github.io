Learning Plan

Planning for success

Long Term Goal

Long term goal resulting from my experience at DevAcademy is to be able to pursue a career in software development/engineering that continues to allow me to enjoy what I like doing.

Strengths and Limitions

    Strengths

  • Harding working
  • Fast learner
  • Determined and invested

    Limitations

  • Sometimes, procrastination
  • Pacing and burnout
  • Reaching out for help

Desired learrning (not technical) I would like to take away

No specific interpersonal skills I can think of, however, I do wish to just learn to enjoy my time more and enjoy what I am doing a bit more. Previously, I have found myself doing things for the sake of just doing it, e.g work, etc.

How will I manage my workload

I usually like to tackle things head on, do it as it comes. I don't have a specific plan in mind, other than just take it one step at a time. I think I might have to remember to tell myself to reach out sometimes, I do find that if I am not prompted I do keep to myself quite a bit. This might come really important especially when things start to build up.

How will I seek help

I would like to see myself open up a bit more. I am trying to make myself reach out and put myself out there more. I saw that change in the first Sprint, being the first one to send an Intro out to the whole cohort, quite nerve-wrecking.

Expectations of the facilitators

My expectations are simple, just a safe and supportive team to make sure I am doing the best I can and willing to step in when needed.

Outside time

As a young adult in today's society, my time commitments remain bare. I like to make sure I am not doing anything work or school related after 5 o'clock as a rule. I do find time to relax and be around my friends and family but nothing is set in stone. I generally take it as it happens but do the regular gaming session, weekend away or just general catch ups.
